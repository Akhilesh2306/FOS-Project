Gold Schema
27 Views Across 6 Domains
Domain 1: Deal Health and Scoring 4 views
  • v_opportunity_health_current - AHP-weighted composite score (0-100) from 5 components
  • v_opportunity_meddicc_breakdown - All 8 MEDDPICC components scored 0-3, gaps identified
  • v_opportunity_stage_velocity - Days in stage vs. benchmarks, velocity status
  • v_deal_risk_indicators - 20+ risk signals aggregated into weighted risk score
Domain 2: Stakeholders and Engagement 8 views
  • v_opportunity_stakeholders_enriched - Contact roles, interaction history, engagement level
  • v_opportunity_activities_rollup - Activity counts, 7d/30d trends, velocity
  • v_contact_interaction_history - Per-contact engagement timeline
  • v_next_contact_recommendation - Contacts ranked by outreach priority
  • Plus 4 simplified variants for performance-sensitive queries
Domain 3: Gong Intelligence 3 views
  • v_opportunity_gong_calls_summary - Call aggregates, Spotlight summaries, quality scores
  • v_opportunity_competitor_intelligence - Competitor mentions from Gong tracker data
  • v_gong_coverage_gaps - Deals with no calls, no transcripts, or stale coverage
Domain 4: Data Quality 3 views
  • v_meddicc_quality_dashboard - MEDDPICC fill rates, coaching priority by stage and deal size
  • v_data_completeness_score - Quality score (0-100), agent confidence level
  • v_stale_deals_detection - Stage-specific staleness thresholds and severity
Domain 5: AI Agent Operations 6 views
  • v_opportunity_llm_context - Complete deal context in one query, under 500ms
  • v_active_recommendations - Currently active NBAs with opportunity context
  • v_playbook_library - Generated playbooks with ratings and usage
  • v_agent_trigger_queue - Pending events with priority scoring and dedup
  • v_agent_recommendation_history - Past recommendations for cooldown and dedup
  • v_rag_content_catalog - Content metadata for Stage 1 RAG filtering
Domain 6: Performance Benchmarking 2 views
  • v_rep_performance_benchmarks - Win rate, deal size, velocity, MEDDPICC avg per rep
  • v_team_activity_benchmarks - Team-level activity baselines by role
