Journey 4

1. Deal Summary

Acme Corp is evaluating Canva Enterprise for their 200-person design team, currently split across Figma and Adobe. The opportunity was created 45 days ago and has progressed through Discovery to Proposal. Key champion is Sarah Chen (VP Design), who is advocating internally but has not yet secured budget approval from the CFO.

Primary risk: Economic Buyer (CFO) has not been engaged. Secondary risk: Figma is actively competing on price. The deal is worth $180,000 annually with potential to expand to 500 seats ($450,000) in year two.

MEDDPICC completion is at 33% (8/24). The strongest areas are Identify Pain (3/3) and Champion (2/3). The weakest are Economic Buyer (0/3), Paper Process (0/3), and Decision Process (1/3).
