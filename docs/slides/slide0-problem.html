Future of Sales
Canva's sales team is flying blind.
Three problems. All connected. All solvable.
1
Fragmented Data
Deal data is in Salesforce. Call recordings are in Gong. Battle cards are in Guru. No single place to see the full picture of a deal.
2
Manual Research
Account Executives spend hours each week pulling up call notes, checking MEDDPICC fields, finding battle cards, and drafting follow-ups.
3
Low Conversion
13 to 15 percent of qualified leads become opportunities. Single-digit close rates. No system tells reps what to do next.
What if the system told every rep exactly what to do, for every deal, every day?
